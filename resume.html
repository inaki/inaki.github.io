Recent Experience

Code for America 2015-present
Frontend Developer San Francisco, CA

I worked with the government of West Sacramento to empower citizen urban farmers with technology.

Alongside a designer and a developer, our team conducted research in the community to develop 3 web applications. For Farmer's I was responsible for the full-stack development, and on Acres I did the design and front-end development.
